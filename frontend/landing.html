The Problem

Existing tools are built for hardware, bandwidth, and budgets you don't have.

3.5 to 5.5 hrs
The real cost to produce a single 60-second short-form video with current tools: scripting, filming, rough cut, colour grade, captions, audio mix, and exporting three aspect ratios. A creator posting 5x/week burns 17 to 27 hours a week on production before an audience ever sees it.

Data Cost

A 1GB bundle can cost 1 to 3 hours of minimum-wage labour in South Africa. Competing browser tools ship 20 to 30MB of WASM codec libraries before they can even process a frame.

20 to 30MB just to load

Hardware

The common local laptop is a dual-core i3-class machine with 8GB RAM, the R4,000 to R8,000 price point actually sold in SA retail. Software video decode chokes this hardware.

R4,000 to R8,000 machines

Pricing

Desktop-native tools run $20 to $60/month (R360 to R1,100). Unaffordable for an early-stage independent creator, especially when priced in foreign currency they can't easily pay.

$20 to $60/month in USD
The Solution

Architecture choices that level the playing field.

iKlippa solves hardware, bandwidth, and pricing barriers at the architecture level, not as an afterthought.

01 / Engine

Rust + WebAssembly

A custom Rust engine compiled to WebAssembly runs entirely in-browser. It handles all video compositing, colour grading, and effects at near-native speed.

Fast on modest hardware
02 / Editor

Custom Browser Editor

A fully custom editor, timeline, colour panel, audio mixer, caption editor, built for buttery-smooth scrubbing on weak hardware. Export never touches a server.

WYSIWYG · instant · private
03 / Director

iKlippa Director (Granite)

A conversational AI co-pilot powered by IBM Granite. Takes plain-language instructions and executes them directly on your timeline in real time.

Creative partner, not a replacement

The technical unlock

iKlippa uses the device's own built-in hardware video decoder (WebCodecs, already in every modern browser) instead of shipping a heavy downloadable codec library.

Features

Built for the reality of short-form creation.

Every feature is designed to speed up the boring parts of editing without taking over your creative decisions.

Afro-Beat Sync

A genuine, culturally-specific differentiator. Snaps cuts to Amapiano and Afrobeat drops automatically, matching the rhythm of the content your audience actually listens to.

Caption Editor

One-Pass Exports

AI Director

Meet your creative partner.

Powered by IBM Granite, iKlippa Director takes plain-language instructions and executes them directly on your timeline in real time. "Remove the dead air." "Add a caption here." "Match this colour grade." Just ask, via chat or slash commands like /trim-silence, /sync-audio, /auto-broll.

AI as a creative partner, not a replacement. It removes drudgery, it doesn't make creative decisions for you.
